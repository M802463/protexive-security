Dog Squads Service

Looking to hire professional Dog Squad services in Mumbai? Whether you're aiming to secure private property, ensure personal protection, or elevate security at a high-profile event, our elite canine units offer the perfect solution.

Our Dog Squads feature pedigreed and expertly trained German Shepherds, Dobermans, and Labradors -each paired with highly experienced handlers. Their presence not only acts as a powerful deterrent but also enhances the prestige and perceived safety of any occasion.

These squads are widely utilized across industrial and corporate environments for perimeter security, facility patrols, building sweeps, area searches, and intruder detection and apprehension.

Professional Security Dog Training

Our dog training program begins with fundamental obedience, ensuring each dog develops the discipline needed to master advanced skills. Specialized command training is delivered using consistent verbal cues that are clear and actionable for the dog.

We focus on managing natural aggression, reinforcing calm responsiveness, and ensuring absolute obedience to handler commands -without hesitation or confusion. Physical training for endurance and agility is also an integral part of the regimen, enabling the dogs to perform tasks with strength and precision.

Each dog is gradually acclimatized to large crowds, preparing them to remain calm, alert, and effective in busy, high-pressure environments.

We have proudly provided Dog Squad services across Mumbai for a wide range of events, as well as for celebrity and VIP protection assignments.
