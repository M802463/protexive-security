Temp Staffing

At times, hiring permanent staff may not be necessary, especially when the requirement is temporary-such as seasonal workloads, new contract commitments, extended staff leave, or reluctance to onboard full-time employees. In such cases, temporary staffing offers the flexibility to scale your workforce without long-term obligations or replacement costs. As a leading contract staffing company in Mumbai, we provide reliable, professional temp staff tailored to your needs. We also offer event staffing solutions, understanding that the success of any event depends on the people behind it. With proven experience in supplying quality staff for corporate events across Mumbai, we ensure you get the right personnel to make your event a success.

We provide Temporary Staffing Service for the post of:

  • Accountant
  • Back office executive
  • Salesman or salesgirl
  • Tele Caller
  • Data Entry Operator
  • Field Staff
  • Event Anchor / Host
  • Information Desk Staff
  • Guest Greeters
  • Exhibition Attendants
  • Parking Assistant
  • Tele-caller

Our manpower service in Mumbai gives you the freedom to hire as per your desire. You can hire manpower in Mumbai on monthly retainer ship basis.
