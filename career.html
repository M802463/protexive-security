HR Policy

Protexive Security Services recruits large number of people every year. We nurture and cherish our employees and they, in turn, relish the opportunity of working with an organisation that takes care of their needs. Working with the Protexive Security Services gives our people experience in a variety of industries, in addition to training in various disciplines. Our employees are expected to adhere to a code of conduct that prescribes the highest ethical standards.

Thank you for taking interest in joining the Protexive Security Services, India’s premier security company. We are an Equal Opportunity Employer. At Protexive Security Services, we just don’t talk about career pathing and development, we DO IT. Throughout the year we have vacancy for security guard job in India for 8 hours and 12 hours. Salary for 8 hours and 12 hours security guard job varies depending upon the location. We have security guard job for night shift and day shift. At Protexive Security Services, you will find a charged and vibrant environment where leaders are developed through constant guidance, counselling and are appreciated on their good performances. We invite you to explore our site and get to know the Protexive Security Services better.

How to reach us?

You can email your resume to HR department on sales@protexivesecurity.com or fill out the below form and attach you latest CV. Inquiry on phone will not be entertained and candidate will be permanently blacklisted. Please do not call us for job openings and inquires.

WE DO NOT ASK FOR ANY UPFRONT PAYMENT FOR ANY JOB OR GIVING OFFER/APPOINTMENT LETTER. Do not make any payment to any person except in our office for uniform. Anyone asking for money upfront for job, please report to us on sales@protexivesecurity.com.

If any amount paid by you to any person including any agent or third party or any person directly or indirectly associated with us, we would not be responsible for it.

To get faster response from us, follow below guidelines:

  • Use only career form for job related enquiries. Do not fill any other form otherwise you would be blacklisted.
  • Attach picture in your Biodata.
  • Mention your actual CTC. If you hike your CTC, you will not get any response from us.
  • Mention your role in previous employment.
  • No. of training hours you have undergone and type of training/certificate you received.

All above points would help us to understankd your skill and expereince based on which we would call you for interview.
