Bank Security

In India, the banking industry pioneered the adoption of professional private security services, recognizing early the need to protect institutions that serve as the backbone of the economy. Today, with financial institutions facing evolving threats, the role of private security has become more vital than ever -safeguarding not just money, but trust.

Modern banks rely on an integrated approach to security. This includes cutting-edge technologies such as CCTV surveillance, intruder alarms, and fortified strong rooms. Yet, it is the presence of trained security personnel -armed or unarmed -that provides the most immediate line of defense. Their watchful eyes, disciplined conduct, and calm authority create an environment that discourages misconduct before it begins.

A guard stationed at a bank is not merely a sentry but a crucial protector of people and assets. Their responsibilities extend beyond vigilance -they must respond decisively in emergencies, manage access control, and maintain a visible deterrent against threats. When faced with critical incidents, prompt coordination with law enforcement is essential, and guards are trained to follow clear protocols, including contacting police without delay.

To ensure our guards are fully prepared, we deliver focused, scenario-based training tailored for the banking environment. From emergency response drills to the use of discreet communication codes during a robbery, our programs are designed to instill confidence and clarity under pressure. Special attention is also given to guarding teller counters and restricted zones, ensuring that unauthorized access is promptly addressed.

In addition, we provide awareness workshops for bank staff, creating a culture of security across the entire branch. These sessions highlight potential risks and offer practical guidance on identifying and responding to suspicious activity. At the heart of it all is our commitment to proactive, intelligent security -ensuring banks remain not just safe, but resilient.


Enhancing ATM Security with Vigilance and Precision

With the growing number of ATM-related fraud incidents, it is imperative that security protocols are followed meticulously. Our on-duty security personnel are trained to enforce the following essential guidelines to ensure the safety and integrity of ATM premises:

  • Individuals must not enter the ATM with their face obscured by helmets, caps, or veils.
  • The use of mobile phones within the ATM enclosure is strictly prohibited.
  • Only one person is permitted inside the ATM at any given time.
  • Extended presence within the ATM without a valid reason is not allowed.

At Protexive Security Services, we understand the critical security demands of banks and ATMs. Our guards are trained to maintain a constant state of alertness, ensuring round-the-clock protection. Using our proprietary mobile application, we monitor the location and movement of deployed personnel in real time, ensuring accountability and responsiveness. Currently, we are proud to serve a growing number of banks and ATMs across Maharashtra, Telangana, and Karnataka.

Please note: In compliance with state regulations, the deployment of armed guards is no longer permitted at banks and ATMs in Mumbai. Only licensed, unarmed security personnel may be assigned for such duties in Maharashtra.
