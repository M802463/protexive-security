Industrial Security

The operations of a manufacturing facility are inherently more intricate than those in the service or trading sectors. In addition to managing complex production workflows, factories must adhere to a comprehensive set of legal and regulatory frameworks, including the Factories Act, Pollution Control regulations, Contract Labour laws, and more.

Security within such industrial environments presents a unique set of challenges. With higher susceptibility to theft, pilferage, and operational disruptions, the need for a robust and intelligent security framework becomes paramount. Effective security is not merely a protective measure -it is a strategic necessity for safeguarding assets, ensuring compliance, and maintaining uninterrupted operations.

Protexive Security Services offers specialized industrial security solutions designed to meet the distinct demands of manufacturing units and warehouses. Our personnel are trained to perform a wide array of essential functions, including but not limited to:

  • Access Control Management
  • Material Movement Oversight
  • Vehicle & Parking Administration
  • Visitor Management
  • Records & Documentation
  • Safety Protocol Compliance
  • Safety Protocol Compliance
  • Routine Patrolling

At Protexive, we don’t just secure spaces -we enable peace of mind through precision, vigilance, and professionalism tailored for the industrial landscape.


In addition to standard responsibilities, our security personnel are trained and equipped to perform a range of specialized tasks, including:

  • Fire Fighting
  • First Aid Administration
  • CCTV Surveillance & Operation
  • Basic Disaster Management

We go beyond conventional security practices to ensure our personnel are well-versed in the operational dynamics of industrial environments. Through rigorous and ongoing training, our guards develop a keen understanding of factory processes, enabling them to proactively identify vulnerabilities and prevent potential losses.

Our approach is both strategic and cost-effective - deploying security teams in a manner that maximizes coverage without burdening your budget. With an acute focus on precision, we tailor our services to meet the exacting demands of industrial and warehouse security. Whether your facility is located in Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, or Bangalore, our team is ready to deliver seamless and intelligent protection.

Specialized Intelligence & Loss Prevention Services

Under our dedicated Detective Division, we offer a discreet Undercover Operative Service - a powerful solution for organizations grappling with:

  • Unexplained stock losses or pilferage
  • Security breaches or employee misconduct
  • Internal theft or vendor collusion
  • Labor unrest, union-related issues, or local interference
  • Any disruptive or suspicious activities affecting productivity
